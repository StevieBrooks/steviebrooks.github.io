The Clubber Meets The Code...

Once upon a time, I was a child of night; sweaty basements, strobe lighting and ridiculous chats with strangers were my thing. In essence, music has always been a big part of my life. Albeit, these days I'm out of bed by 5:30 am for a hard day's study as if I'm one of the Essenes. I've always been a creative individual but the world of copywriting for other entities has finally taken its toll. These days, I would rather write my own obituary.

A hodgepodge of catalysts has caused me to invest in myself and make this career transition. It wasn't a tough choice, however, coding itself is more difficult than quitting smoking. I should have tried to learn Mandarin instead. Quips aside, this journey into tech is arming me with the tools I need to make my contribution to the world. Ideas plague my mind as I've never been able to implement most of them. I'm sincerely hoping that a robust knowledge of programming will help. Anyway, this is me, a bubbly and light-hearted soul ready to try and make a difference. Now it's time to hit the code!
